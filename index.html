Welcome to Codegiya edulab

Codegiya EduLab is an online educational platform initially designed to support students and teachers at Kamuzu Barracks Community Day Secondary School. The platform offer a comprehensive collection of free educational resources focused mainly on Computer Studies and Physics for MSCE students.

Our Key Features:

  • Detailed subject handouts.
  • Practice quizzes.
  • Textbooks and reference materials.
  • Past papers.

Currently specializing in Computer Studies and Physics, we plan to expand our resource library to cover more subjects within the JCE and MSCE curriculum.


Whether you're a student looking to enhance your understanding or a teacher seeking quality educational materials, our user-friendly platform makes it easy to access the resources you need for success in your academic journey.

About Us

Codegiya edulab was founded by Allan Chafukira, a passionate educator and developer. With a vision to bridge the gap in educational resources, Allan created this platform under the codegiya brand to provide high-quality, easily accessible materials for students and teachers across Malawi.
