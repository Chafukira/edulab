Welcome to Codegiya edulab

Codegiya EduLab is an online educational platform designed to support students and teachers at Kamuzu Barracks Community Day Secondary School and other schools across Malawi. We offer a comprehensive collection of free educational resources focused on Computer Studies and Physics for MSCE students.

Our Key Features:

  • Detailed subject handouts.
  • Practice quizzes.
  • Textbooks and reference materials.
  • Past papers with detailed solutions.
  • Well-organized content structure.

Currently specializing in Computer Studies and Physics, we plan to expand our resource library to cover more subjects within the MSCE curriculum. Whether you're a student looking to enhance your understanding or a teacher seeking quality educational materials, our user-friendly platform makes it easy to access the resources you need for success in your academic journey.

About Us
